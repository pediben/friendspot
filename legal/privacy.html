Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Friendspot ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Friendspot, we may collect:

  • Account information — your phone number, display name, and optional profile photo.
  • Contacts — with your permission, we access your phone contacts only to show which of your contacts are already on Friendspot. We do not store your full contact list on our servers; we only store hashed phone numbers to match existing users.
  • Messages and voice notes — messages and voice notes sent inside Spots are end-to-end encrypted. We cannot read them.
  • Usage data — basic analytics like which features you use, to improve the app.
  • Device information — device type, OS version, and push notification token to deliver notifications.
  • Payment handles — if you choose to save your Venmo username or PayPal email, these are stored in your profile and visible only to members of your Spots.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Friendspot service.
  • To match you with friends already on the app (using hashed phone numbers).
  • To send push notifications about activity in your Spots.
  • To support you if you contact us with questions or issues.

3. End-to-End Encryption

Voice notes and circle messages sent in Spots are encrypted on your device before being uploaded. Only members of a Spot who hold the circle key can decrypt and read those messages. Friendspot servers never have access to the plaintext content of your encrypted messages.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers — Supabase (database and authentication), Expo (build infrastructure), and LiveKit (voice rooms). These providers process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.
  • Law enforcement — only when required by law or a valid legal process.

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can request account deletion by contacting us at hello@friendspot.online. Upon deletion, your profile and messages are removed within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Friendspot is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us immediately.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and download your personal data.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@friendspot.online.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including TLS in transit, AES-256 encryption for voice notes, and Supabase Row Level Security to ensure only authorized users can access their data.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email. Continued use of Friendspot after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at hello@friendspot.online.
