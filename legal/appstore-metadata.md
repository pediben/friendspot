## Description (4000 chars max)

Friendspot is the private social app built for real friend groups — not followers, not algorithms, just the people who actually matter.

**Spots — Your Private Group Hub**
Create a Spot for any group: your camping crew, college friends, family, or fantasy league. Each Spot is a private space with voice rooms, shared expenses, moments, and more — all in one place.

**Drop-in Voice Rooms**
Jump into a live voice room with your Spot anytime. No scheduling, no video — just talk like you're in the same room. Start a private call for smaller conversations.

**Split Bills & Settle Up**
Track who paid for what in your group. Split expenses, run ROSCA savings rounds, place friendly bets, and settle up directly through Venmo or PayPal with one tap.

**Moments**
Create shared events for trips, birthdays, dinners — anything worth remembering. Add photos, track expenses, and keep a secret planning mode so the honoree stays in the dark until the big reveal.

**Planning Lists**
Shared checklists inside every Spot. Perfect for camping trips, road trips, and group dinners. Assign items so everyone knows their part.

**End-to-End Encrypted Voice Notes**
Send voice messages inside your Spots. Everything is encrypted on your device — Friendspot servers never see your content.

**Direct Messages**
One-on-one messages with anyone in your Spots.

**Stories**
Share moments with your circles — posts expire in 24 hours.

---

Everything in Friendspot is private by design. No public profiles. No strangers. No algorithmic feed. Just your actual people.

## Notes for App Review
- Voice rooms require a custom dev build (uses LiveKit native SDK)
- Contacts permission: used only to match hashed phone numbers with existing users. No contacts are uploaded or stored.
- The "Simulated Gambling" feature (Bets) uses in-app coins only — no real money is exchanged within the app.
- Payment settlement (Venmo/PayPal) opens the third-party apps via deep link — no money flows through Friendspot.
