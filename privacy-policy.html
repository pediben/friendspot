Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

Friendspot ("we", "our", or "us") is a private social app that lets you create spaces for your closest friends and family. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. When you sign up, we collect your phone number or email address to create and authenticate your account. You may also provide a display name, username, profile photo, bio, job title, and company name.

Content you create. We store voice notes, direct messages, photos, stories, events, expenses, bets, and other content you post inside your Spots.

Contacts. With your permission, we access your device's contact list to show you which of your contacts are already on Friendspot. We do not upload your full contact list to our servers — we only check for matches.

Device and usage data. We collect your device push notification token to send you alerts. We do not use third-party analytics tools.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the app
  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure
  • To deliver messages, voice notes, and notifications to you and your Spot members
  • To show you which contacts are on Friendspot (with your permission)
  • To process Pro subscription purchases

We do not sell your personal information. We do not show ads.

3. End-to-End Encryption

Voice notes are end-to-end encrypted using ECDH P-256 key exchange and AES-GCM encryption. Only members of a Spot can decrypt and listen to voice notes. We cannot access the contents of encrypted voice notes.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share your information only with:

  • Supabase – our backend database and file storage provider
  • LiveKit – real-time audio infrastructure for voice rooms
  • Apple – for push notifications via APNs
  • Expo / EAS – for app distribution and over-the-air updates

We do not share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third parties.

5. Data Retention

Stories are automatically deleted after 24 hours. Other content is retained until you delete it or delete your account. You can delete your account at any time from the Profile screen in the app.

6. Children's Privacy

Friendspot is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. California Privacy Rights (CCPA/CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information we collect, to request deletion of that information, and to opt out of the sale of their information. We do not sell personal information. To submit a request, contact us at pedi.ben@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the new policy in the app or by other means. Continued use of Friendspot after changes take effect constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
